About Us

Purpose of the Tourism Website

At Kiwi Trails and Tales, our mission is to connect travelers with the true heart of New Zealand. Beyond just sightseeing, we want our visitors to experience the stories, culture, and natural wonders that make Aotearoa unique. Every path, coastline, and mountain holds a tale, and our goal is to bring those tales to life so every adventure leaves a lasting memory.

Who We Are

We are a passionate team of local guides, storytellers, and nature enthusiasts who have explored every corner of New Zealand. With a deep respect for the land and its history, we aim to share the magic of our country with travelers from around the world. Our team combines expertise, creativity, and a love for adventure to ensure each journey is immersive and unforgettable.

Experiences We Highlight

From guided hiking trails and cultural workshops to coastal cruises and culinary experiences, Kiwi Trails and Tales offers a wide range of activities. Whether it's learning Maori traditions, spotting native wildlife, or enjoying adrenaline-filled adventures, our tours blend education, entertainment, and exploration. Each experience is crafted to create meaningful connections between travelers and New Zealand's rich heritage.

Our Philosophy

We believe that travel is more than just visiting places, it's about understanding the stories behind them. Every tour we offer reflects this philosophy: connecting people to the land, the culture, and the communities they encounter. By combining storytelling, local knowledge, and immersive experiences, we hope every traveler leaves not just with photos, but with a deeper connection to New Zealand and its spirit.


Our Mission

To create immersive New Zealand experiences that go beyond sightseeing, connecting travelers with the land, culture, and stories that make Aotearoa truly special.

Our Story

Born from a passion for adventure and storytelling, our team of local guides brings New Zealand's hidden gems and legends to life, sharing them with travelers from around the globe.

Why NZ?

From majestic mountains and pristine coastlines to rich Maori heritage, New Zealand offers a unique blend of natural beauty and culture. Every journey with us uncovers its magic in a way you'll never forget.
